About Us

At BALGRAM, we are committed to providing a safe haven and comprehensive support for vulnerable children in need. Our Child Care Institutute offers refuge and care for children facing various challenges,
including runaway situations, trafficking, homelessness, and exploitation.

We believe in providing a nurturing environment where every child's well-being is prioritized. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that each child receives the support and attention they deserve. From providing basic necessities to offering counseling and educational opportunities, we strive to empower children to overcome adversity and thrive.

Our shelter home Started since 2006 is a place of refuge and hope for children who may have nowhere else to turn. We provide a range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each child,
including access to healthcare, educational support, recreational activities, and vocational training.

Whether a child is in crisis due to natural disasters, living in unauthorized areas, or facing social marginalization, BALGRAM is here to offer compassionate care and a supportive environment. We work closely with local authorities and community partners to ensure that every child receives the assistance they need.

Join us in our mission to protect and nurture vulnerable children. Together, we can make a meaningful difference in their lives and create a brighter future for all. Contact us today to learn more about our Child Care Institutute and how you can support our efforts to provide care and protection to children in need.
