Welcome to BALGRAM

Bal Gram Rai, Sonipat has been running since 1980 under the domain of Women and Child Development Department,
Panchkula Haryana . It provides all types of facilities to the Orphan, abandoned,
Surrendered and CNCP(Child in Need, care and Protection ) children.

All the Staff Members are well qualified and appointed after duly
Verification by the Police.
